Overview

  • Bivariate copulas models
  • Vine copula models

In all examples, it is assumed that using namespace vinecopulib; is used. While the namespace vinecopulib contains the most important functionalities described below, there are a two others that are available to the user:

  • bicop_families: convenience definitions of sets of bivariate copula families
  • tools_stats: utilities for statistical analysis
